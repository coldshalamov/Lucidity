## Apple App Store

### Promotional Text (170 chars)
Control your WezTerm terminal from anywhere. Pair with a QR code, type on your phone, see results instantly. Secure, fast, and always in sync.

### Description
Lucidity lets you access your desktop terminal (WezTerm) from your iPhone or iPad. Whether you're on the couch, in another room, or across the world, your terminal is always at your fingertips.

**How It Works**
1. Run WezTerm on your computer.
2. Press Ctrl+Shift+L to show the pairing QR code.
3. Scan the QR with Lucidity on your phone.
4. Approve the connection on your desktop.
5. Start typing! Your keystrokes appear in real-time.

**Key Features**
• **Instant Pairing**: Scan a QR code—no accounts, no complex setup.
• **Real-Time Terminal**: See output as it happens with minimal latency.
• **Secure by Design**: End-to-end encrypted connections. Your data never stored on our servers.
• **Works Anywhere**: Local network or over the internet via our secure relay.
• **Full Keyboard Access**: Send Ctrl, Alt, Escape, arrow keys, and more.
• **Multiple Panes**: Switch between open terminal panes on your desktop.

**Requirements**
• WezTerm with Lucidity features enabled (see our GitHub for setup).
• iOS 14 or later.

**Privacy**
We do not log or store your terminal data. Ever. See our Privacy Policy for details.

## Google Play Store

### Short Description (80 chars)
Control your desktop terminal from your phone. Secure. Instant. Everywhere.

### Full Description
Lucidity brings your desktop terminal to your Android phone. Paired with WezTerm, you can view and interact with your terminal sessions from anywhere.

☑️ **Easy Setup**
Scan a QR code displayed on your desktop. Approve the connection. Done.

☑️ **Real-Time Sync**
Keystrokes and output flow instantly. Feels like you're typing directly.

☑️ **Secure**
Connections are encrypted end-to-end. No terminal data is ever stored on our servers.

☑️ **Anywhere Access**
Use over local Wi-Fi or the internet via our secure relay server.

☑️ **Developer Friendly**
Full access to Ctrl, Alt, Tab, Escape, arrow keys. Perfect for SSH, coding, and server management.

**How to Get Started**
1. Install WezTerm on your computer (wezfurlong.org/wezterm).
2. Enable Lucidity features (see our GitHub).
3. Open Lucidity on your phone.
4. Scan the QR code shown on your desktop.
5. Start typing!

**Open Source**
Lucidity is open source. Check out the code on GitHub: github.com/lucidity-app/lucidity

**Feedback**
We'd love to hear from you! Report bugs or suggest features via the app or email beta@lucidity.app.
